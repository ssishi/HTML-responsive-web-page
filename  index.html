Drakkentech is a young web development and software engineering company based in Cape Town that creates complete solutions in a variety of development fields, including data management, process automation, reporting, client relationship management, enterprise resource management, and smaller, more general web applications and websites.

Their charges are really competitive for a young firm, and they work hard to establish themselves. They strive to improve South Africa for all people and help with this by giving prospective developers who might not otherwise have any job opportunities both mentoring and job opportunities.
